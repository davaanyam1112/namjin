Who is Mr.Dorjnamjin?

I am Mr. Dorjnamjin Nyamsuren, a young Mongolian (27-year-old). I studied International Economics and Relations at National University of Mongolia and Utica College in New York as a U.S State Department funded Global UGRAD Scholar respectively. Having ten years' experience in community services, where I served as a member of UN Youth Advisory panel, Head of National Children Council, "Child Vision" under World Vision Mongolia, and Founder of "World Leadership" International Youth Network.

I invest in crypto and stocks, and co-founded my own small token project called SDB “Sustainable Development in Blockchain. I lead tours with my own travel company called “Mongolian Top Trips Co.,Ltd” since 2016. I am also an author of a book named "Mongolian Development Model-TORUS" published in 2017. Making initiatives a reality and working with international team, and doing something that makes others happy is what I am passionate about. Since 2020, I have been an adviser at “EducationUSA Mongolia”, which is funded by the State Department of the U.S. This is an educational project implemented by Mongolian Association of State Alumni (MASA).

My values, beliefs, attitudes, ideas all are devoted to making life better for billions of people in future through establishing myself as a "smart creative" in the world of business.
